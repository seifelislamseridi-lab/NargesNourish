Our story

Narges means daffodil — the first bloom of spring.

We named the kitchen after that first flower because it stands for fresh starts and natural goodness. Our mark turns the bloom into a bowl: a flower you can eat, a meal that helps you grow.

Every subscription is built the same way — real, whole ingredients, prepared fresh each morning, rotated weekly so you never get bored, with nothing artificial hiding in the recipe.
